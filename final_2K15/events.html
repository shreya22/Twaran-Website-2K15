BLESSED IS THE ONE WHO GETS AN OPPORTUNITY!

Going on with the above saying, IIITM presents TWARAN, a chance to DEFEND TILL THE END!

TWARAN, literally meaning momentum, gives the students an opportunity to showcase their talents in various undermentioned games:

formal events:


CRICKET (boys)
BADMINTON (boys & girls)
VOLLEYBALL (boys & girls)
LAWN TENNIS (boys)
TABLE TENNIS (boys & girls)
SQUASH (boys)
BASKETBALL (boys & girls)
ATHELETICS:
MARATHON (boys)
SPRINT 400m (boys & girls)
SPRINT 800m (boys & girls)
HURDLES RACE (boys & girls)


informal events:


COUNTER STRIKE
CHESS
CARROM
