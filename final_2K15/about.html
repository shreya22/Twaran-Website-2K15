About TWARAN..


TWARAN is the national level inter college sports fest of ABV-IIITM Gwalior. Born taking in mind a small but enthusiastic motive of encouraging sports among youth, TWARAN has seen expansive participation and electrifying sportsmanship, and the heat felt grows every year..
Under the guidance of our honourable Director Sir Prof S. G. Deshmukh, our Faculty co-ordinator Dr. Anurag Srivastava TWARAN 2K15 is all set to fuel the energy within.
Twaran is a three-day sports mania in ABV-IIITM Campus. Each day witnesses various formal + informal events in various rounds starting from the knock-out to the quarter finals to semi finals and ultimately the finals. keeping the trend, TWARAN 2K15 is back!!
The concluding day will be made even more exhilarating by rock band performances! followed by a DJ night :)!

So guys be ready! Revoke the sportsman within, and pack up to beat the chill this JAN.. IIITM Family welcomes you..
