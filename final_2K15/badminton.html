BADMINTON


A new game called Mintonette evolved as a pastime to be played preferably indoors and by any number of players.
The game took some of its characteristics from tennis and handball.
Hand pumping lobs and elegant smashes make this game a jubilant watch and equally entertaining for the cheering viewers.
